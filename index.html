Summary

The Georgetown Plaza renovation project is a forward-looking initiative aimed at modernizing and revitalizing a long-standing commercial space. Our vision focuses on creating a more dynamic, visually appealing, and functional environment that better serves the needs of our tenants and the surrounding community.

With sustainable design practices, updated architecture, and an emphasis on accessibility and efficiency, the remodel aims to position Georgetown Plaza as a landmark destination for business and leisure in Stockton.
